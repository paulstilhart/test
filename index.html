Welcome to the ICE flow

Once upon a time... In a remote area of the North Pole, thought to be abandoned, the village of a little hero called Eskimoz. The sun shines every day of the year, the ice pack is sparkling and pollution does not exist. Eskimoz lives by hunting and fishing, sharing new adventures with his friends, a mutant northern panda always there to help, a funny Penguin always there to make a good joke, a hummingbird, a faithful adviser, a polar bear who keeps order and many other emblematic characters.

This village is the place we all dream of, a simple society, where judgement does not exist, where everyone can do what they want, when they want, without suffering or worrying about any pressure. This world is far from excessive consumption, far from ego, being the best in this country is simply being yourself without feeling guilty.

But beware, this territory could end up coveting the jealousy of some... The jealousy of those who have everything but can't be as happy.
